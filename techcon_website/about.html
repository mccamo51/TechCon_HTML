Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots initiative by a group of passionate developers and tech educators. Over the years, it has evolved into a global conference, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to empower and inspire individuals in the tech industry by fostering learning, innovation, and collaboration. We aim to provide a platform where diverse voices and ideas shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Recognized as one of the first computer programmers, Dr. Lovelace shared her insights on the future of computational theory during TechCon 2018.

Alan Turing

A pioneer in artificial intelligence and cryptography, Alan Turing’s work was featured in a keynote celebrating the evolution of machine learning at TechCon 2020.

Grace Hopper

Rear Admiral Grace Hopper inspired audiences at TechCon 2022 with her revolutionary work in computer programming and her advocacy for women in technology.
